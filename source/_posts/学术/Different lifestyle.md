**Little A is called super scholar, Little B is called inferior student.**


Little A is called super scholar, Little B is called inferior student.


Little A is a hard-working student. Little B is an active student. Little A likes simple and pure life. Little B likes various and challenging life. They comes from different places and don't know each other but in the same university. Every time when Little B drinks coffee with his friends at the cafe, Little A is studying. When Little B plays sports, Little A plays C program. When Little B watches film in the city center, Little A is reading books in the library. When Little B enjoys his rest of time exploring new fields by thinking and communication, Little A is deepening his study by doing thousands of exercises. Sometimes they all worry about themselves. Little B worries about keeping balance between work and life. Little A sometimes feels depressed or stressful. However, Little B thinks he gets precious experience while Little A thinks he finds more valuable and invisible things.



One day, these two men meet together. They don’t understand each other’s lifestyle so when they communicate, they find it hard to reach an agreement on many things and they nearly argue to fight. A fewdays later or a week later, maybe a couple of months, a year, a life, will they still hold the same opinions on the opposite?
